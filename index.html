Bio

I am a final-year PhD candidate in the Computer and Information Engineering Department at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ), supervised by Prof. Junfeng Wu and Prof. Zhikun Zhang. I am currently a visiting scholar at Emory University, working with Prof. Li Xiong. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2021.

My research interests lie in machine learning privacy and security, including differential privacy, privacy-preserving federated learning, and the emerging privacy and security challenges of large language models (LLMs) and LLM-based agents.

I am actively seeking full-time research opportunities in academia and industry and would welcome any relevant discussions.
